Spicy Boneless Buffalo Wings

Boneless Buffalo Wings bite-sized appetizer or snack, that are spicy and delicious. The sauce can be switched up to make them spicy, savory, or sweet. Other sauces can be BBQ, Teriyaki, or Chipotle Rance.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 12 oz buffalo sauce
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 425 degrees
  2. In a large bowl combine flour, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. In a small bow whisk egg and milk together
  4. Cut chicken breast into 1/2 inch chunks and coat with egg mixture. Then coat with flour mixture.
  5. In batches, place chicken into air fryer and cook for 15-20 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, heat hot sauce and butter in a skillet.
  7. Place cooked chicken in sauce mixture and toss to coat.
